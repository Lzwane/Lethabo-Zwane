Pharmacy Laboratory Accident

Feb 15 , 2024

A laboratory incident occurred the School Of Pharmacy New Lab. The incident involved a chemical exposure that affected pharmacy third year students. During a scheduled laboratory session in the Pharmaceutical Lab of SOP, students were conducting experiments involving a certain chemical. Unfortunately, later after the practical session, an unexpected incident occurred, resulting in students experiencing certain reactions to the chemical, specifically affecting their eyes leading to their immediate need for medical care.

We are pleased to inform you that most students have shown significant improvement and have fully recovered. This is mainly because we ensured that all affected students received the proper medical attention befitting of their condition. We would like to first thank everyone who shared their heartfelt messages to the BPharm 3s and wished them well during this difficult period. The support that was demonstrated by the SMU community and other external members was truly overwhelming and did not go unnoticed.
