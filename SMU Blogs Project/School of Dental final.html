World Oral Day

March 20, 2024

The School of Dentistry at Sefako Makgatho Health Science s University will celebrate World Oral Health Day on March 20, 2024, with the theme "A Happy mouth is A Happy body". The purpose is to raise awareness that prioritizing oral health leads to better overall health, and to educate people on preventing oral diseases and maintaining good oral health. Poor oral health is linked to serious medical conditions like pneumonia, heart disease, and pregnancy complications. To improve oral health, the school encourages everyone to follow simple rules: take care of your mouth daily, eat a healthy diet, avoid tobacco, brush and clean your teeth regularly, and visit a registered oral health practitioner twice a year. By doing so, oral diseases like dental caries, periodontal diseases, and tooth loss can be prevented, ensuring a beautiful smile and overall good health.

Memorandum To The School of Dentistry

May 15 , 2024

Here is a shortened version of the text: The School of Dentistry is addressing the impact of a recent gas leak/disruption in the dental laboratories and clinical areas. After consultation, it was determined that the gas line is irreparable and unsafe. To minimize disruption to the academic year, the Academic Planning Committee has implemented the following measures:

- Adjusted academic timetable for affected groups (BDS 2 & 3) to continue practical training at an off-site facility
- Transportation will be provided and a travel roster will be drawn up
- Interim arrangements for BDS 4 & 5 to use shareable handpieces
- Timetable adjustments will be communicated through HODs, DSC, and Class representatives
- Students should continue with scheduled academic sessions
The School is working to find a long-term solution to replace the damaged gas line.
