About Us

SMU is inspired by the legacy of Sefako Makgatho, a courageous leader, educator and 2nd president of the African National Congress.
SMU is one of three newly established post-apartheid universities embodying all the ideals of an inclusive,
non-racial, non-sexist, transformed and democratic South Africa.
