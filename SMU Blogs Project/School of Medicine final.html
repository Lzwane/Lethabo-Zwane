School of Medicine Update

May 14 , 2024

The SOM council held a meeting with class representatives on April 24, 2024, to address various issues within the school. Updates on these issues include:
- Dosimeters for Brad 2, 3, and 4 students to detect radiation exposure are being procured, and students will not have to pay for them.
- Reduction in clinical sites for Brad students is being addressed through collaboration with private sectors and acquisition of new sites.
- Issues with Biophysics in Brad 4 are being mitigated through meetings with the Department.
- Medicine 1, 2, and 3 issues, including releasing of Nursing staff and doctors, DH situation, formalin concentration, and fees, are being addressed and escalated to University council and DHET for funding.
- Deadlines for resolving outstanding issues are set for May 13, 2024.
The SOM council is engaging with departments to address departmental issues and ensure student concerns are heard and resolved.

Passing of Jason Debeila

May 11, 2024

It is with great sadness to announce the passing of Jason Debeila, a final-year MBCHB student, on May 11, 2024, at Dr George Mukhari Academic hospital after a long-term illness. He will be remembered for his strong and positive spirit. The School of Medicine extends condolences to the Debeila Family, his classmates, and the SMU student community. Memorial service and funeral arrangements will be communicated soon. May he rest in peace. "The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit" (Psalm 34:18).
