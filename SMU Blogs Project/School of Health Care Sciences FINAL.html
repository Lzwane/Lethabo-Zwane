Nursing Science Department & Fundraising Run

April 17, 2024

Nursing students organized a successful fundraiser, where attendees were required to pay a R70 entrance fee and bring non-perishable food items. The event saw a great turnout, with a significant amount of food and funds collected. The proceeds will go towards supporting struggling students, providing them with much-needed assistance to continue their studies. This initiative not only demonstrated the nursing students' commitment to helping others but also showcased their ability to make a positive impact in their community.


The Department of Physiotherapy motivates FTENs during Prize-Giving eremony

March , 2024

The Physiotherapy Department welcomed First-Time Entering Students (FTENs)

with a prize-giving ceremony, emphasizing the importance of conduct and delivering results. Top-performing students were awarded, and academic and support staff were introduced. The ceremony concluded with FTENs receiving souvenirs for choosing to study physiotherapy.
