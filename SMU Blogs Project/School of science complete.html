UPDATE ON NSB AIRCONDITIONING AND MAINTANANCE

Title description, Dec 23, 2024

The SST Council has received several and various complaints from students in the school about the issue of air-conditions at the NSB especially in the labs, students had even resorted to bringing in their own smaller-nyana fans just to ensure they get some air

The SST council as the custodian of students took the matter seriously and escalated the matter to chief operating officer. We are pleased to inform you that the university has aquired air-conditioners and installations are underway.The council remains dedicated to assisting students of school

From a delivery boy to a PhD graduate

Dr Abongile Bele's story of perseverance, Sep 2, 2024

Dr. Abongile Bele, born on April 14, 1996

Eastern Cape, overcame a challenging childhood to achieve a PhD in Physics at 28. Despite poverty and tragedy, he excelled in studies, working part-time from age 13 to support his family.His research on mixed-phase nanophosphor materials has led to groundbreaking findings,
and he is now a lecturer and mentor, inspiring others to pursue STEM fields. Dr. Bele's story is a testament to determination and hard work,inspiring young people from disadvantaged backgrounds to strive for greatness.

Students enhance their application of Mathematics in IT and 4IR

May, 2024

A workshop on computer science and technology was

held for postgraduate students, covering applications of mathematics in IT and the Fourth Industrial Revolution. Experts shared knowledge on AI, game development, and programming, with hands-on activities. Students gained a foundational understanding of computer science and explored career paths, engaging with potential employers and identifying real-life math applications.
